## Stakeholder Request

**_We’d like to get a clearer view of how our fulfillment network is operating across distribution centers and regions. Can you take a look at order activity and fulfillment flow and help us understand how things are performing across the network, and whether there are any patterns we should dig into further?_**

## Initial Questions

### Scoping Questions

1. **What exactly is meant by order activity and the fulfillment flow?**
2. **What business decisions will this analysis support?**
3. **Who is the primary audience for this analysis, and what level of detail do they require?**
4. **Is all the necessary data available to perform this analysis?**
5. **What key metrics and dimensions are needed to answer the stakeholder’s request?**
6. **Over what time period should order activity be analyzed—last month, last quarter, or last year?**
7. **Should all distribution centers (DCs) and regions be compared equally, or should the focus be on specific ones where performance is a concern?**
8. **Are there any specific product categories that require deeper analysis?**
9. **Are there any key performance thresholds that should be used as benchmarks for service levels and processing times?**
10. **How is a “delay” or a “bottleneck” defined in this context?**
11. **Should the analysis primarily focus on identifying general trends, or also highlight exceptional cases?**
12. **How is the cost of return measured?**

### EDA Questions

1. Breakdown order processing times into creation -> shipped, shipped-> delivered, delivered->returned by region, DC(s), products, product categories, timeline(day,month)
2. Order Volume by region, DC(s), products, product categories, timeline(day,month)
3. Revenue by region, DC(s), products, product categories, timeline(day,month)
4. Breakdown queries to find out whether products need to be reallocated to different DC(s)
